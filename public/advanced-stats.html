Stats Glossary

wRC+
Weighted Runs Created Plus. Measures overall offensive value relative to league average. 100 is average; higher is better.
xwOBA
Expected Weighted On-Base Average. Based on quality of contact; removes luck from hitting results.
FIP
Fielding Independent Pitching. ERA-like stat based only on strikeouts, walks, and home runs.
xFIP
Expected FIP. Normalizes home run rate to league average to isolate pitcher skill.
xERA
Expected ERA based on quality of contact allowed. A good indicator of whether ERA will rise or fall.
Barrel%
Percentage of batted balls hit with ideal exit velocity and launch angle.
Hard-Hit%
Percentage of batted balls with exit velocity of 95 mph or higher.
BB%
Walk rate. Higher is better for hitters, lower is better for pitchers.
K%
Strikeout rate. Lower is better for hitters, higher is better for pitchers.
WHIP
Walks plus Hits per Inning Pitched.
DRS
Defensive Runs Saved. How many runs saved versus an average defender.
OAA
Outs Above Average. Statcast-based fielding metric.
