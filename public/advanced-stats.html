Stats Glossary

wRC+
Weighted Runs Created Plus. Measures overall offensive value relative to league average. 100 is average; higher is better.
xwOBA
Expected Weighted On-Base Average. Based on quality of contact rather than outcomes; removes luck from hitting results.
FIP
Fielding Independent Pitching. ERA-like stat based only on strikeouts, walks, and home runs. Strips out defense.
xFIP
Expected FIP. Normalizes home run rate to league average to further isolate pitcher skill.
xERA
Expected ERA based on quality of contact allowed. A good indicator of whether a pitcher ERA will rise or fall.
Barrel %
Percentage of batted balls hit with ideal exit velocity and launch angle. High barrel rate means loud contact.
Hard-Hit %
Percentage of batted balls with exit velocity of 95 mph or higher. Measures consistent hard contact.
BB%
Walk rate. Higher is better for hitters, lower is better for pitchers.
K%
Strikeout rate. Lower is better for hitters, higher is better for pitchers.
WHIP
Walks plus Hits per Inning Pitched. Measures how many baserunners a pitcher allows per inning.
DRS
Defensive Runs Saved. Estimates how many runs a player or team saves versus an average defender.
BsR
Base Running Runs. Measures overall baserunning value above average.
